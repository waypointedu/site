No Login Required

Waypoint cohorts share materials through guided Google Drive folders, weekly emails, and scheduled capstone conversations. There is no separate LMS to sign into.

How you access your courses

  • Weekly email briefing: Faculty send readings, practice exercises, and the capstone prompt for each module.
  • Shared Google Drive: Every cohort receives a drive with folders for readings, submission templates, and capstone instructions.
  • Google Meet links: Faculty mentors host orientation and capstone check-ins; recordings are saved privately for assessment when needed.
  • Capstone workspace: Peer groups upload their audio or video conversations for faculty review—no grades are posted publicly.

Privacy & language support

  • Students may use pseudonyms and audio-only meetings if that helps them participate freely.
  • Capstone recordings can be submitted in your native language; we translate on receipt.
  • Need printed materials or alternate delivery? Let us know and we will mail or courier what is permissible.

Need help getting connected?

Email admin@waypoint.institute or use the contact form. We can resend drive links, troubleshoot Google access, or arrange alternative delivery.

If you are waiting on cohort placement, watch for an onboarding email with your introduction seminar schedule and technology walkthrough.

About
