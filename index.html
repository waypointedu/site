Programs at a glance

BF-1

Certificate in Biblical Foundations & Spiritual Formation

Seven modular courses with supplemental apologetics seminars. Finish self-paced or in a guided one-year schedule.
More coming soon

Next certificates in development

  • Certificate in Culture & Media
  • Certificate in Mission & Apologetics

Complete any two certificates for an associate-equivalent. Add roughly 1.5–2 more years of coursework to reach a bachelor-equivalent. Expect 2-week orientation seminars and 16-week courses, with flexible pacing so most finish a certificate within a year.

Secure Cohorts

Instruction is in English. For certain secure cohorts, we provide short written summaries in Dari or Pashto when a bilingual facilitator is available. Pseudonyms are welcome; no recordings are posted.

Who we serve

  • Christians and seekers worldwide who need serious formation without cost.
  • Learners in restricted contexts (e.g., international seekers, freethinkers, and Christians) who require privacy and caution.
  • Lay leaders and bi-vocational ministers seeking structured study.
