Give to Waypoint

Students pay nothing. Donors underwrite first-run courses and the cohorts that follow. Your generosity makes serious Christian formation accessible to learners worldwide—including those in restricted contexts.

Ways to Give

$100

Fund a Cohort Leader

Support a table leader for one 8–10 week cohort serving 12–15 students. Covers stipend and training materials.

  • Sustains one small group
  • Provides pastoral guidance
  • Ensures quality facilitation
Most Popular
$500

Fund a Brand-New Course

Underwrite a first-run course with live faculty instruction. Recordings (where safe) are reused to scale learning for years.

  • Creates reusable content
  • Covers faculty honorarium
  • Scales to multiple cohorts
$2,500

Fund Half the Pilot

Support 2–3 courses and multiple cohorts. Make a significant impact on the pilot semester's reach and sustainability.

  • Funds multiple courses
  • Supports 5–7 table leaders
  • Reaches 60–100 students
$5,000

Fund an Entire Semester

Underwrite all 5 pilot courses, cohort leaders, and site costs. Enable Waypoint to serve 120–200 students this semester.

  • Covers full pilot semester
  • Includes site and tech costs
  • Maximum impact
