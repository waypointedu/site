How we train table leaders

  • Complete the course they will lead (audit or certificate track)
  • Attend a 2-session orientation on facilitation and pastoral care
  • Receive course-specific teaching notes and discussion guides
  • Join ongoing peer supervision and support

Stipend support

  • $100 per cohort (8–10 weeks) funded by donors
  • Recognizes the time and care table leaders invest
  • Ensures accessibility for bi-vocational leaders
  • Sustains quality without burdening learners
