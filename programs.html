Programs & Certificates

Modular certificates that you can complete one course at a time. Each finished course appears on your Waypoint Transcript.

CS-1

Certificate in Christian Studies

Build a foundation in Scripture, doctrine, moral theology, and church history. Designed for laypeople and ministers seeking structured theological formation.

Core outcomes:

  • Navigate the narrative arc of Scripture with canonical awareness
  • Articulate Nicene Trinitarian and Christological doctrine
  • Engage virtue ethics and Christian moral reasoning
  • Trace key themes in church history from the Apostolic era to the Reformation
CM-1

Certificate in Culture & Media

Learn to read culture theologically and engage media with discernment. Ideal for those navigating digital, entertainment, and public culture.

Core outcomes:

  • Develop a theological framework for analyzing culture
  • Examine religion's representation in popular media
  • Explore theories of meaning, symbol, and narrative
  • Apply frameworks to case studies in contemporary media
MA-1

Certificate in Mission & Apologetics

Equip yourself for witness, apologetics, and pastoral care—especially in complex or restricted contexts. Combines theory with practicum.

Core outcomes:

  • Survey major world religions with theological awareness
  • Practice apologetics that respects conscience and avoids coercion
  • Navigate evangelism and pastoral crises under pressure
  • Complete a supervised practicum in witness or service

Tracks & Workload

Audit (light)

Participate in weekly discussions and complete readings. No assignments required. Receive a certificate of participation.

  • 90-minute weekly session
  • Assigned readings
  • Discussion participation
  • No formal assessment

Certificate (standard)

Complete 3 short assignments plus a final reflection. Graded Pass/No-Record. Appears on your Waypoint Transcript.

  • All audit components
  • 3 brief written assignments
  • Final reflection paper
  • Pass/No-Record grading
