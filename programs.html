Waypoint Certificate in Biblical Foundations & Spiritual Formation

This one-year certificate program equips students to grow in the knowledge of God, His Word, and His world. The program balances hermeneutics, Old and New Testament study, doctrine, culture, and spiritual practices, with apologetics integrated throughout. Learners can follow the typical one-year pathway or move at a self-paced speed to match family and ministry rhythms.

Pathways

Expect a 2-week orientation seminar before beginning 16-week courses. Cohorts are paced together, but learners can work flexibly—many finish a certificate in under a year while others take additional time as needed.

Complete any two certificates for an associate-equivalent pathway. Add roughly 1.5–2 more years of coursework after the certificates to reach a bachelor-equivalent.

BF-1

Certificate in Biblical Foundations & Spiritual Formation

Seven 16-week courses (4 cr each), plus a 2-week orientation (1 cr) and an integrated apologetics seminar (1 cr). Total 30 credits. Self-paced; one-year typical.

Tracks & Workload

Audit (light)

Participate in weekly discussions and complete readings. No assignments required. Receive a certificate of participation.

  • 90-minute weekly session
  • Assigned readings
  • Discussion participation
  • No formal assessment

Certificate (standard)

Complete 3 short assignments plus a final reflection. Graded Pass/No-Record. Appears on your Waypoint Institute Transcript.

  • All audit components
  • 3 brief written assignments
  • Final reflection paper
  • Pass/No-Record grading
